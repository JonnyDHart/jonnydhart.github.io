About Me

Hi I’m Jonny, a game designer with a Bachelors of Science in Game Design from Full Sail University.

I've always been driven by a love for video games and storytelling - inspired by the way interactive worlds can pull you in and challenge you. To me, games can be more than entertainment; they're one of the most powerful tools we have for teaching, training, and understanding.

I believe great games can educate, heal, and connect - from helping players explore complex topics like mental health, to turning ideas into memorable, hands-on experiences. My goal is to design games that entertain first, but also leave a lasting impact long after the screen fades.
